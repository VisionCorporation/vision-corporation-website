At Vision Corporation (“we,” “us,” “our”), we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, deliver personalized content, and analyze website traffic. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how our website (“Site”) uses them, the types of cookies we use, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

1.1 Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

1.2 Cookies help websites operate more efficiently and provide important information to website owners.

1.3 Cookies allow our Site to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our pages.

2. Cookies Used on Our Site

2.1 We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and ensure the proper functioning of our website.

2.2 Cookies on our Site may be used to:

  • 2.2.1 Ensure the website operates correctly.
  • 2.2.2Remember your preferences and settings.
  • 2.2.3Enhance performance and user experience through usage analytics.
  • 2.2.4Personalize content and advertisements based on your interests.
  • 2.2.5Improve website security and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • 3.1 Necessary Cookies

    • 3.1.1Essential for core site functionality such as page navigation, secure login, and form submissions.
    • 3.1.2These cookies do not require user consent because they are crucial for the website’s operation.

  • 3.2 Performance Cookies

    • 3.2.1Help us understand how visitors interact with the website.
    • 3.2.2Collect information anonymously on frequently visited pages and user behavior.
    • 3.2.3Enable us to optimize website performance and usability.

  • 3.3 Functional Cookies

    • 3.3.1Allow the Site to remember user choices (e.g., language, region, or personal settings).
    • 3.3.2May be used to provide enhanced or personalized features.
    • 3.3.3Support functions such as loading videos or saving form inputs.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

4.1You may choose to accept or decline cookies at any time.

4.2Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings.

4.3Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and performance of our Site.

4.4If you decline cookies, some features or pages may not work as intended.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

5.1We may update or modify this Cookie Policy periodically.

5.2Updates may occur due to changes in our services, technology, or legal requirements.

5.3When changes are made, the updated version will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
