At Vision Corporation(hereinafter refered to as “we” “us”, “our”), we use cookies to improve your browsing experience, deliver relevant content, and track website traffic. This cookie policy explains what cookies are, how our Website (“Site”) use them, the types of cookies we use and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

Cookies allow the website to recognize your device. This helps us to remember your preferences and shows us how you interact with the web pages.

Cookies used on our Site

We uses cookies to improve your experience on our website, including and without limitation, to:

  • Ensure that our website functions properly.

  • Remember your preferences and settings.

  • Improve performance and user experience by understanding how you use the website.

  • Personalize content and advertisements relevant to your interests.

  • Enhance website security and prevent fraudulent activity.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies

    • These cookies are essential for you to browse the website. Without these cookies, services like secure login, form submission or page navigation cannot be provided. Consent is not required for these cookies as they are strictly necessary for efficient site function.

  • Performance Cookies

    • These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Information on the pages you often visit is collected and reported anonymously. This data helps us optimize our website and make it easier for you to navigate.

  • Functional Cookies

    • These cookies enable the website to remember choices you make, such as your name, language or the region you are in, and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may also be used to provide services you have requested, such as watching a video.

Managing Your Cookie Preference

You are able to accept or decline cookies. Through your browser settings, you can decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and performance of our site

Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may modify or update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Such amendments might be necessary due to the need to keep the Cookie Policy up to date or because of changes in Vision Corporation’s services, technology or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
