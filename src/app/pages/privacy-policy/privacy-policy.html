At Vision Corporation ( hereinafter referred to as “we, “us”, “our”), we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or communicate with us.

What information do we collect?

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonable be linked, directly or indirectly, with you(“Personal Information”).

  1. Information You Voluntarily Provide

    This is information that you actively share with us when interacting with our services, such as:

    • Contact Data: Your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.

    • Account Data: Username, password, and other registration details.

    • Communication Data: Records of correspondences with us such as testimonials, support inquiries, and feedback.

  2. Information Collected Automatically

    When you visit our website, certain data is collected automatically to help us understands user behaviour and improve performance:

    • IP address, browser type and device information.

    • Pages viewed, time spent, and navigation patterns.

    • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (as explained in our Cookie Policy )

How do we use your information?

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services

  • For security and fraud prevention

  • To communicate with you

  • For marketing and promotion with consent

How and why do we share your information?

We do not sell your personal information. We may share you information only in the following circumstances:

  • Trusted Service Providers: We engage trusted third-party companies and individuals to perform services on our behalf (e.g., Web hosting, data analytics, email deliver, CRM Software.) These third parties are authorized to use your Personal Information only as necessary to provide these services to us.

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in good faith believe that such an action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect the rights or property of us, or protect the personal safety of users or the public.

  • Your Consent: We may share your information with any other party with your explicit consent or at your direction.

How do we protect your information?

We use industry-standard security measures to keep your data safe, including:

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections.

  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only.

  • Regular monitoring and updates to our system to prevent data breaches.

How long do we keep your information?

We will not retain your personal data for any longer than is necessary for achieving the purpose(s) outlined in this policy or as authorized by the Data Protection Act, 2012(Act 843) and any other applicable local law.

Your Data Protection Rights

You may have the following rights regarding your Personal Information:

  • The right to request copies of your Personal Information.

  • The right to request corrections to any information you believe in inaccurate or incomplete.

  • The right to request that we erase your Personal Information under certain conditions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Such amendments might be necessary due to the need to keep the Privacy Policy up to date or because of changes in Vision Corporation’s services, technology or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
