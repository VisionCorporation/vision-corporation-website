We care about your privacy

Discover how Vision Corporation collects, uses, and protects your personal information. This page explains our commitment to safeguarding your data, maintaining transparency, and ensuring that your privacy choices are respected across all our services.

Last Updated [November 23, 2025]

At Vision Corporation (“we,” “us,” “our”), we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent, lawful, and secure manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies or can be reasonably associated with you (“Personal Information”).

1.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

This includes information you actively share with us when using our services.

  • 1.1.1 Contact Data
    • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • 1.1.2 Account Data
    • Username, password, and other registration details.
  • 1.1.3 Communication Data
    • Records of communications, testimonials, support inquiries, and feedback.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information is automatically collected to help us understand user behavior and improve performance.

  • 1.2.1 Technical Data
    • IP address, browser type, device information.
  • 1.2.2 Usage Data
    • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, navigation patterns.
  • 1.2.3 Cookies & Tracking Data
    • Cookies and similar tools (see our Cookie Policy for details).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your Personal Information for the following purposes:

2.1To provide, operate, and improve our services.

2.2For security, fraud prevention, and system monitoring.

2.3To communicate with you regarding updates, inquiries, or support.

2.4For marketing and promotional purposes (only with your consent).

3. How and Why We Share Your Information

We do not sell your Personal Information. We may share your information under these circumstances:

3.1 Trusted Service Providers

We may share data with third-party companies that assist us in delivering services, such as:

  • 3.1.1Website hosting providers
  • 3.1.2Data analytics tools
  • 3.1.3Email delivery systems
  • 3.1.4CRM and support software

These partners are only permitted to use your information to provide services to Vision Corporation.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to:

  • 3.2.1Comply with applicable laws or legal processes
  • 3.2.2Protect our rights or property
  • 3.2.3Protect the safety of users or the public

3.3With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties only with your explicit consent.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data.

4.1Secure servers and encrypted connections.

4.2Limited access to Personal Information by authorized personnel only.

4.3Continuous monitoring and system updates to prevent breaches.

5. How Long We Keep Your Information

5.1We retain your Personal Information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

5.2Retention is in accordance with the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) and other applicable laws.

5.3Once data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights:

6.1The right to request copies of your Personal Information.

6.2The right to request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.

6.3The right to request the deletion of your Personal Information under certain conditions.
